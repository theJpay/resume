What defines me

Forged through battles

Adaptivity

During a match, I have to constantly think about what I should do in order to win. But my opponent won't wait for me. Therefore, I have to take the right decision in a blink of an eye, reacting to what my opponent is doing with the right gesture and winning point after point, decision after decision.

Competitivity

A competition is never won or lost before actually doing it. That's why every time I do something, I give my best. Even if my opponent is one point away from victory I will continue to believe that I can comeback and try to surpass myself. Every defeat that I experienced were lessons that I learned and helped me to be better, in fencing, but also at work.

Teamwork

Fencing is never learned alone. And it is the same at work. Even if what I produce myself is what I did, the ideas, the practice and the reflections all come from the people around me. I couldn't be a good fencer without all my partners around me to train with and helping me improve my qualities and erasing my flaws.
